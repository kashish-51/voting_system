What is Chunav?

Chunav is an online voting system which helps you to shift from traditional ballot system to the mysterious world of internet. With the help of this system you do not have to stand in long queue and you can save your time too. Now you can vote anytime, anywhere at the comfort of your place.

Our Features

Easy and convinient

It becomes easy to vote when you use Chunav.

Secure

It does not reveal the identity of voter and safe in every possible way.

Accessibility

You can use it anytime-anywhere.

Affordable

To use this system all you need is a good internet connection.

Free of Cost

It is completely free. You do not need to pay a single penny to use it.

Fast results

As soon as the voting ends it shares results and statistics with you in a fraction of seconds.
